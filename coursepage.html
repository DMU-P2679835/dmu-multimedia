Our Course

Programme: International English Program

At SWU

As you are aware, our course areas include two years of study in Thailand and two years of study in England. We will focus on basic electrical engineering theory subjects throughout these 2 years. Physics of sound and Acoustic Materials were a specific subject in the first year, the two are intertwined and allow us to employ them in studio design projects.

In the second year, we’ll begin to focus on more concert-related topics. There is a subject that requires us to come up with one concert theme and give a presentation as if we were presenting to our client, and Concert Safety subject that teach us how to design a concert venue by considering the factors that cause accidents and teaches how to make a concert manual document.

At DMU

We will learn media production or how to use media to present our work. such as live broadcasts via TV or radio, creating apps and websites through coding or designing tools, creating sound from scratch for a short audio story in audio production class, understanding how social media works and how to use it for marketing purposes, and how to create video and image through editing and insert effects. And DMU has equipment and software such as Adobe to make World-class productions for students to borrow and use. Furthermore, the DMU Placements Team will assist students in applying for internships or positions at well-known companies or institutions.
